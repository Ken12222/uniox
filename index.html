Our comprehensive enterprise solution covers 360 degrees of your business, streamlines processes, boosts efficiency, and drives sustainable business growth.

People Management
  • Core HR: Manage employee records, organizational charts, job positions, onboarding, and offboarding.
  • Payroll: Automate payroll calculations, tax deductions, compliance, and direct deposits.
  • Talent Management: Streamline recruitment, performance management, and succession planning.
  • Time and Attendance: Track employee time, manage leave, calculate overtime, and create schedules.
Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Contact Management: Maintain detailed customer profiles and contact history.
  • Lead Tracking: Monitor and manage leads through the sales pipeline.
  • Sales Pipeline: Visualize and track the progress of sales opportunities.
  • Feedback Surveys: Gather and analyze customer feedback to improve services.

Analytics Management
  • Data Collection: Gathering relevant data from different modules of the ERP system, such as finance, sales, inventory, HR, and operations.
  • Data Integration: Combining data from various sources to provide a comprehensive view of business operations. This might involve integrating external data sources with the ERP system.
  • Data Analysis: Using statistical methods, machine learning, or other analytical tools to analyze the collected data. This helps identify trends, patterns, and correlations that can provide insights into business performance.
Financial Management
  • General Ledger: Multi-currency support, charts of accounts, journal entries, financial reporting, budgeting and Period closing
  • Accounts Payable: Vendor management, invoice processing, payment processing, and expense management.
  • Accounts Receivable: Customer management, invoice tracking, payment processing, and collections.
  • Fixed Assets: Manage acquisitions, depreciation, transfers, disposals, and maintenance tracking.
  • Tax management: Automated tax calculations, compliance and reporting
Procurement Management
  • Supplier Management: Manage supplier relationships, purchase requisitions, order management, and goods receipt.
  • SRM: Monitor supplier performance, manage risks, and handle contracts efficiently.
